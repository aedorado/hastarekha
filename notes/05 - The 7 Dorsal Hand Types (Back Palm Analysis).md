# Hasta Sāmudrika Śāstra: The 7 Dorsal Hand Types (Back Palm Analysis)



## Introduction to Back Palm Analysis

While the palmar (front) side gives us the 4 basic elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water), the dorsal (back) side of the hand provides a different layer of classification. 

* **The Western Division:** Western palmistry divides hands into 8 to 11 types. To avoid confusion, this system streamlines them into **7 practical types**.

* **The Importance of Nails & Skin:** You cannot make a final prediction based *only* on the shape of the dorsal hand. The texture of the skin (soft/hard) and the shape of the nails (wide/small, long/small, wide/big) play a massive modifying role in the final reading.



---



## 1. The Elementary Hand (Primitive / Labor Hand)



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Looks thick, heavy, and stiff. The palm appears highly prominent, resembling a **square** (driven heavily by a prominent Mars mount). The fingers look less prominent compared to the massive space of the palm. The wrist is thick and wide. Nails are typically small.



* **Skin Texture:** Usually hard, rough, and stiff. It lacks flexibility (e.g., trying to force a small bangle onto a stiff wrist). It looks like skin that is not pampered or moisturized. 

* **Core Mentality (Short-Term Survival):** Known as the "labor hand," their mindset is strictly focused on the present day: "Work hard, eat, drink, and sleep." They do not plan for the future, nor do they save for their children's futures. 

* **Lack of Ambition:** Even if they work in a high-end environment (like a nice boutique) and possess hidden skills, they refuse to invest in themselves. If offered a chance to learn and advance, they will decline, preferring their basic daily routine.

* **Emotional Profile:** Low emotional sensitivity and low appreciation for creativity. If a child proudly shows them a painting, they will flatly say, "Okay, I made it too, now let me sleep."

* **Life Struggle:** Because they refuse to plan or upgrade their skills, their life is inherently full of struggle. Even if they have good lines on their palm, the "soil" (the hand itself) is thick and rocky, meaning success requires grueling physical labor.



## 2. The Spatulate Hand (Spoon Hand)



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Shaped like a spatula or a spoon (flaring out wider at the top or bottom). Similar to the Elementary hand, but the wrist is noticeably **thinner**, the skin is less rough, and the nails are not as small.



* **The "Best" Category:** This is considered one of the best hand types because it balances extreme physical capability with mental sharpness. 

* **Skill & Advancement (Learn, then Earn):** Unlike Elementary hands, they recognize their own skills and actively seek to improve them. Their core formula is: **Learn first, then make money from that learning.**

* **Discipline & Independence:** They are practical, hardworking, but fiercely independent. They strictly follow rules and time boundaries. 

    * *Example:* If their shift ends at 5 PM, they will leave exactly at 5 PM. If they have a specific prayer time (like Friday Namaz), they will not waste a single minute on other tasks; they are highly disciplined. This discipline is tied to the strength of their Thumb (Willpower).



## 3. The Square Hand (Vertical Hand)



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> The entire hand (palm + fingers combined) visually forms a square. Even if mathematically rectangular, the visual "look" is square. Nails often naturally take a square shape as well.



* **The Business Category (Earn without Learning):** Also placed in the "best" category. Unlike the Spatulate hand (which must learn a skill to earn), the Square hand possesses an innate, natural business acumen. They can figure out how to make money and run businesses *without* prior formal learning. 

* **Balanced Personality:** They perfectly balance emotions with practicality. They are excellent, long-term planners (crucial for business).

* **Determination vs. Stubbornness:** While the Earthy/Elementary hand is blindly stubborn, the Square hand is highly *determined*. They are flexible enough to pivot if needed, but relentless in their goals.

* **Social Grace:** They possess excellent social etiquette—they know exactly how to sit, speak, present themselves, and handle people in a room. They often work for social welfare (donating money, sharing ideas, or teaching for free).

* *Modifier:* If a Square hand has small nails, they develop a researcher's mindset (investigative) but may suffer minor health issues.



## 4. The Conical Hand (Conic Hand)



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Long and thin hand. The fingers are long, thin, and taper smoothly forward. The middle finger is generally notably long. The nails are usually beautiful.



* **Luxury & Moodiness:** They deeply desire a luxurious, comfortable life and generally attain it. However, they are highly moody. Their emotions fluctuate rapidly, and they have very little patience for minor inconveniences. 

* **The Broad Definition of Creativity:** They are highly creative, but creativity is not just drawing. 

    * *Analogy:* In the Mahabharata, stating "Ashwatthama is dead (the elephant or the human?)" was a creative use of communication. Speaking ambiguously, managing networks cleverly (like a student staying logged into class while setting up a new router), or hosting guests beautifully are all forms of Sun/Mercury creativity. *(Astrological link: 3rd House Buddha, 5th House Sun).*

* **Settlement Issues:** They struggle to "settle" in foreign environments. They absolutely hate staying in other people's houses (even their own mother's house), vastly preferring the neutral comfort of a hotel or their own private space.



## 5. The Philosophic Hand (Knotty Hand)



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Similar to the Conical hand (long and tapering), but features prominent, visible **knots** at the finger joints. 



* **The Finger Modifier:** Knots do not have to appear on every finger. If knots appear only on Jupiter and Saturn, the philosophical traits will apply strictly to the domains of those planets.

* **Sub-Categories:**

    1. *Simple Knotty:* Standard philosophical mind.

    2. *Thin Skinned:* Nerves are visible through the back of the hand. Adds extreme sensitivity to the philosophical nature.

    3. *Crooked Fingers:* (Like the sage Ashtavakra). The finger that is crooked amplifies its specific planetary energy in a distorted/intense way.

* **Core Mentality (The Message Deliverers):** They apply deep philosophy to everything they do. 

    * *Real-World Example:* Found in character actors who only choose roles with deep hidden messages (e.g., Naseeruddin Shah, Sushant Singh Rajput), stand-up comedians, and motivational speakers. They abhor mindless entertainment (like just dancing to a pop song).

* **Particularity & Secrecy:** They are highly particular about their comfort ("I will only eat from *this* specific plate"). They limit their space, preferring a specific quiet corner where they remain calm and secretive.



## 6. The Psychic Hand



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Extremely long, delicate, and "super soft." Looks like the hand of a royal princess or a newborn baby—so fragile you are afraid to squeeze it.



* **Extreme Sensitivity:** They are physically and emotionally fragile. They catch colds/coughs easily and have weak stomachs. Emotionally, if you do not praise them, their mood is ruined. Like a "touch-me-not" plant, they break easily under pressure.

* **The Spirituality vs. Laziness Divide:**

    * *With Knots:* They can ascend to the absolute highest levels of spirituality and healing.

    * *Without Knots:* They become trapped in their comfort zone. They refuse to move (will not even get up to fetch a plate of food). Any minor change to their daily routine sends their mind into chaos.

* **Line Coloration:** A true psychic hand should have very light, fine lines. If a super soft hand has thick, dark lines, it indicates a tragic paradox: a delicate person forced to endure a harsh, struggling life. 



## 7. The Mixed Hand



> [!INFO] Core Identification

> Does not perfectly fit any of the above 6 categories. It is a blend. 



* **How to Read:** Identify the "Major" type first (e.g., "This is mostly a Square hand, but with Conical long fingers"), then blend the traits.

* **Core Mentality:** They are ultimate multitaskers and full of diverse ideas. They seamlessly blend practicality, emotion, and creativity. 

* *Note:* To predict their exact career path, you must rely heavily on reading the actual lines of the palm and fingerprints, rather than just the hand shape.

## Technical Palmistry Rules & Notes



### Photography Rules for Clients

1. **Morning Swelling (Water Retention):** Never read a hand from a photo taken in the morning if the client suffers from water retention. Swelling artificially distorts the shape of the mounts and fingers.

2. **Nail Polish Prohibition:** Clients must completely remove all nail polish (even transparent coats). A palmist must read the fine vertical and horizontal micro-lines physically present *on the surface* of the nail to gauge health and planetary shifts. Polish fills in and hides these lines.



### Determining Risk-Taking Ability

Risk-taking is not determined by the back of the hand alone. It requires analyzing:

1. The Square hand base (gives the foundation for risk).

2. The Thumb (Willpower to execute).

3. The Jupiter and Sun fingers (Ambition and desire to shine).

> - **Analysis:** Clear Square hand with short nails. Possesses a remarkably strong Mercury (Buddha) finger, indicating high business acumen and communication.

> - **Detail:** Arvind wears a ring on this finger based on a priest's advice, which the speaker notes is working well for him. 



> [!example] Case 3: Dravin's Hand (Square + Mixed)

> - **Analysis:** Initially misidentified by students as Spatulate or Elementary. 

> - **Correction:** The speaker identified it as a Square palm with Mixed fingers. The hand is remarkably soft with very long fingers, looking almost like a female hand if viewed in isolation.

> - **Key Feature:** He possesses an unusually massive Mercury (Buddha) finger, indicating an incredibly sharp mind hiding behind a "simple/innocent" appearance. 



> [!example] Case 4: Gopal's Hand (Elementary)

> - **Analysis:** An Elementary hand with a very small thumb.

> - **The Exception Rule:** Despite the small thumb (usually indicating low willpower/speed), the *upper phalange* of the thumb is highly swollen/fat. This specific swelling explains why he is always the fastest person to type messages in the class group chat. 



> [!example] Case 5: Abhijeet's Hand (Square)

> - **Analysis:** Initially, some thought it was Elementary. The speaker corrected this: the nails are large/round, the skin is soft, and the fingers indicate strong business understanding. It is firmly a Square hand.
