# Back Hand (पृष्ठ हस्त) Classification — Cheiro's/Western System

> [!INFO] Context
> Earlier sessions covered the **four types of hands from the front palm (front pam)** view. This session moves to **back-hand (peeth pam / bैक pam)** classification — attributed to **Cheiro**, hence also called the **Western Division**.
> - Some traditions read back-hand types in **8 categories**
> - Some in **11 categories**
> - A condensed/simplified version reads it in **7 categories** — this is the version being taught here.

> [!WARNING] Reading Difficulty from the Back
> When viewing a hand from the back (peeth), at first glance it is **harder to interpret** than the front palm. This is because:
> - **Nakhun (nails)** play a **major role** in back-hand reading — long/short, wide/narrow, big/small nails all change the result.
> - **Skin quality** (soft vs. hard) on the back of the hand also alters the reading.
> - Therefore, **no single feature should be treated as a final/end result** — every other factor (skin, nails, mounts, lines) must be cross-checked together before concluding a reading.

## 1️⃣ Sequential Lecture Notes

### A. Elementary Hand (एलिमेंटरी हस्त / श्रमिक हस्त — "Labourer's Hand")

> [!EXAMPLE] Sanskrit/Hindi name
> **श्रमिक हस्त (Śramik Hasta)** = Elementary Hand

**Physical characteristics:**
- Compared to other hand types, this hand appears **thicker / heavier (bhārī-bharkam)** — not a thin hand.
- Viewed from the back, the **palm (hatelī) portion appears prominent**; the fingers do **not** look as prominent as the palm — i.e., the palm visually dominates the hand.
- The palm covers **more area**, giving a **square-shaped palm** appearance.
- **Contributors to the squareness:**
  - The role of the **Mars mount** (this part).
  - The role of another adjoining zone (referenced but not fully detailed — teacher pointed to a live hand example, Prītu jī's, where this area was notably prominent, "coming out like a bulge").

**Skin quality (2 sub-types identified):**
| Sub-type | Skin quality |
|---|---|
| Type 1 | **Soft skin** |
| Type 2 | **Hard/rough (kaṭhor) skin** |

> [!NOTE] Typical/"True" Elementary Hand
> The **classic/original Śramik Hasta** typically has **hard, rough (kaṭhor, rūkh-sī) skin** — like skin that never gets cream/moisturizer, often due to circumstances such as manual/warm-climate labour work.
> - Can also be understood via **body type correlation** — a stiff (rigid) body type often accompanies this stiff hand type.

**Flexibility test:**
- Compare to a flexible hand (e.g., one that a bangle slides onto easily) vs. a **stiff hand (kaṛā hāth)** where a bangle goes on with difficulty.
- The **Elementary Hand base is "difficult to form"** — i.e., it is thick, wide, and hard, and the **wrist (kalāī)** is **wider** than a normal/thin wrist.
- The **thumb (aṅgūṭhā)** is typically **thick**.

> [!TIP] Important Distinction — Thickness vs. Mount Quality
> Teacher explicitly clarified: *"I said the thumb is thick — I did NOT say which specific mount (parva) is thick."*
> - The **quality of the parva (segment/phalanx)** is a **separate** consideration from the overall thickness of the digit. Do not conflate the two.

**Mentality / psychological profile of Elementary Hand people:**
> [!WARNING] Repeated Emphasis — Low-ambition mindset
> Speaker repeatedly stressed [emphasis: stated multiple times] the following traits:
> - Their thinking is: *"Just get today's roṭī (bread); today's work is done — I don't think about what comes next."*
> - This is **not necessarily manual/labour work** — could even be someone working at a good boutique or a good place, but they **won't use their own skill to grow**. They may possess a genuinely good skill but have **no interest in advancing** with it.
> - Even when **offered opportunities** ("do this and you'll do better," "think about tomorrow," "invest here") — **they show no interest**. Their attitude: *"I ate, I drank, I slept today"* — and this **increases the struggle in their life**.
> - **Line & hand quality can both increase or decrease life's struggle.** Even if their lines (rekhāyeṅ) are good, the desired outcome will only come **after a lot of hard labour** — hence **hand quality is the first thing to check**, before even lines.
> - Their mentality is termed **"nimna soch"** (lower/limited thinking).
> - They typically **lack real recognition of their own talent/skill** — don't realize *"this skill I have — the world is progressing with it, I could do this too."*
> - **No ambition to progress.** If shown something admirable, they won't even praise it. Example given: if their own child brings something to show them ("Papa, look!") — [conversation trails off, implying lack of enthusiastic response].
> - Contrast with **practical people**: practical people *do* have some attachment/interest in the future and think ahead — Elementary Hand people **do not**.

**Gender note:**
- This hand type is **not exclusive to males** — females can have thick, heavy Elementary hands too.
- In females with this hand: they can be **hard-working**, but tend to show **lower emotional/caring attitude and sympathy (samvedanā)**.

### B. Square Hand (चौकोर / स्क्वेर हस्त)

> [!NOTE]
> Discussed briefly via a live example (see **Hand Examples** section — Prītu jī). Key markers used to identify Square Hand:
> - Nails **not** short (short nails would exclude this category)
> - Palm shape appears **square** from the back
> - **Buddhi finger (Mercury finger)** good → indicates good **business acumen (vyāpārik samajh)**

> [!TIP] Sun mount & 5th house parallel
> While discussing creativity in a Square-Hand example, teacher connected it to **Jyotiṣa**: creativity is assessed via the **3rd house (Buddhi), 5th house (Pañcham Bhāva), and the Sun** — every person has creativity, but it manifests differently depending on planetary influence.
> - A **strong Sun quality in the hand** → desire to do things **"with perfection,"** not superficially.
> - Such people may enjoy **good food** — like both **cooking well** and **hosting/serving guests well** (ātithya/hospitality quality).
> - **Settling down difficulty:** Square-hand-influenced (Sun-quality) people often find it **hard to settle in one place**. This is heightened if their nature becomes **overly soft/moody** or **overly stubborn (ziddī)** — e.g., preferring a hotel alone over staying at a relative's home, even one's mother's house.

### C. Philosophical Hand (फिलोसोफिकल हस्त) — also called Knotty Hand (नॉटी हस्त / गाँठों वाला हस्त)

> [!EXAMPLE] Two names, same category
> **Naughty/Knotty Hand = Philosophical Hand** — same category, two names.

**Visual confusion with Conical Hand:**
- At first glance, Philosophical Hand and **Conical Hand** look similar — **both have thin fingers**.
- **Key differentiator = knots (gāṅṭheṅ)** at the finger joints, visible especially from the back.
  - A hand **without prominent knots** → looks very soft, smooth (example: Prītu jī's hand had no raised knots).
  - A hand **with raised/prominent knots** at the joints → **Knotty/Philosophical Hand**.

> [!IMPORTANT] Knots may appear on select fingers only
> It is **not necessary that all knots are equally prominent**. Whichever **finger(s)** show more prominent knots, the **quality of "knottiness" (naughty finger quality) manifests more strongly through those specific fingers.**
> - Example given: If the **Jupiter finger** and **Saturn finger** show raised knots → the naughty-finger quality will show up **more in Jupiter & Saturn** significations, rather than in Sun or Mercury finger significations.
> - Overall hand quality remains **almost the same**, but **only specific fingers carry the "naughty/knotty" trait**.

**Three sub-categories of Philosophical/Knotty Hand:**

| Sub-category | Description |
|---|---|
| 1. Simple Naughty Finger | Basic knotted finger joints, otherwise normal |
| 2. Thin-skin variant | **Very thin skin** — veins (nas) visible through the back of the hand. Predictions shift slightly when veins are visible; indicates very soft skin underneath. |
| 3. Crooked/uneven shape variant | The **shape of the hand/fingers becomes crooked (ṭeṛhā-meṛhā)** — e.g., fingers bending sideways (teacher compared it to how a body can appear twisted, as in certain conditions/postures — "जैसे अस्थमा पर पूरा शरीर टेढ़ा था" reference). Example shown live: Jupiter finger bent/crooked, knot protruding outward. |

> [!TIP] Effect of a crooked finger
> Whichever finger is crooked, the **"crookedness" impact (ṭeṛhepan wālā impact)** is localized to the significations of **that specific finger**.

**Personality & profession associations:**
- Philosophical/Knotty Hand people are often seen among **actors**, particularly those who do **meaningful/message-driven acting** — not mainstream commercial roles (e.g., dance numbers to Punjabi songs). Example referenced: a deceased actor known for a "dark," limited, message-driven filmography — later clarified by a participant as **Sushant Singh Rajput** (noted as having died of suicide/cancer — speaker was uncertain and asked the group to verify).
- Their movies/creative choices tend to carry a **hidden message**.
- **Stand-up comedians** frequently show this hand type — creativity increases, and the **Moon (Chandra) quality** in the hand also increases.
- **Stubbornness (ziddī)** can be very high.
- **Correlation:** The more **crooked** the finger AND the more **soft** the hand's skin → the more **stubborn/rigid in preference (mūḍī)** the person becomes (e.g., insisting on eating only from a specific plate).
- Contrast: **Elementary Hand people** are the opposite — they'll eat off anything, even a leaf-plate, without fuss.
- Knotty-finger people tend to be **calmer and more secretive (śānt, secretive)** compared to Conical Hand people — Conical types are not as calm/secretive.

### D. Psychic Hand (साइकिक हस्त)

> [!NOTE] Definition
> Psychic Hand = an **intensification** of both **Conical** hand quality AND **Philosophical/Knotty** hand quality — i.e., it amplifies both.

**Physical trait:**
- Extremely **soft palm** — described as feeling like a **newborn baby's hand** (soft enough that one hesitates to press it, "डर लगता है").
- The **softer** the hand, the **more sensitive** the person — sensitivity extends to:
  - Nature/personality
  - Emotional responses
  - Even **susceptibility to illnesses**

**Spiritual potential — CONDITIONAL:**
> [!IMPORTANT] Condition for high spirituality
> - **IF** knots (gāṅṭheṅ) are present in a Psychic Hand → the person **can rise to a very high level of spiritual attainment (adhyātma kā uchtar sthān)** — possibly a **high-level spiritual personality**.
> - **IF NO knots** are present → the person tends to **prefer their comfort zone**, avoiding physical exertion — described humorously as wanting food served without having to get up.

**Line quality:**
- In Psychic Hand people, lines (rekhāyeṅ) tend to appear in a **lighter colour**.
- (Contrast note: In **Elementary Hand**, lines are typically **thick**, not fine — line thickness/colour to be studied in a later class.)

**Career/behavioral implication:**
- Psychic Hand people who dislike leaving their comfort zone can do the **same job for years without change ("sālon-sāl ek jaisā kām")**.
- **Practical advice given:** Give such a person a **fixed, defined routine job** ("this is your work, this is your routine") — they will follow it reliably. But **any change** to that routine can cause significant mental disturbance ("dimāg idhar se udhar āsmān tak pahuchne jaisā").

### E. Mixed Hand (मिश्रित हस्त / मिक्स्ट हैंड)

> [!NOTE] Why this category exists
> Sometimes a hand **doesn't clearly fit** any of the five preceding categories (Elementary, Square, Conical, Knotty/Philosophical, Psychic) — in such cases it is classified as **Mixed Hand**.

**Method of analysis:**
1. First check overall **skin quality** (soft/hard) for a baseline read.
2. Identify a **"Major Type"** — i.e., the dominant category the hand resembles most (e.g., "looks mostly like Square Hand").
3. Then note **deviating features** — e.g., major type = Square, but nails are short (an Elementary/other-type trait), or fingers are somewhat long (a trait from yet another type).
4. **Add the qualities of the secondary hand type(s)** onto the major type reading progressively.

**Personality traits of Mixed Hand people:**
- Capable of **multi-tasking**, because they carry qualities of **two or three hand types** combined.
- Can be **practical, emotional, AND creative** simultaneously.
- Often show **indecision in career** ("should I go this way or that way?") because multiple directional pulls exist.
- Tend to be **full of ideas** ("bahut sāre ideas se bhare hue").
- Best predicted through **detailed line reading**, layered on top of the hand-type reading.

## 2️⃣ Technical Summary

### Key Definitions & Terminology
| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| पृष्ठ हस्त / Back/Peeth Pam | Hand viewed from the back — basis of Cheiro's/Western classification |
| श्रमिक हस्त (Śramik Hasta) | Elementary Hand ("labourer's hand") |
| नॉटी हस्त / फिलोसोफिकल हस्त | Knotty Hand = Philosophical Hand (same category) |
| साइकिक हस्त | Psychic Hand |
| मिश्रित हस्त | Mixed Hand |
| गाँठें (Gāṅṭheṅ) | Knots at finger joints — the defining feature distinguishing Philosophical/Knotty from Conical hand |
| कड़ा हाथ (Kaṛā Hāth) | Stiff hand (rigidity test via bangle-fitting analogy) |
| परवा (Parva) | Finger phalanx/segment — its *quality* is distinct from overall finger *thickness* |

### Interpretive Rules & Principles
- **No single feature = final verdict.** Nails, skin quality, mount elevation, and line quality must ALL be cross-verified before concluding a reading — this applies especially to back-hand readings, where nails have outsized influence.
- **Hand quality precedes line quality** in determining life's "struggle level" — check hand type/quality *first*.
- **Knots on specific fingers** localize the "knotty" personality trait to that finger's significations only (not the whole hand uniformly).
- **Skin softness is proportional to sensitivity** — softer skin = more emotionally/physically sensitive.
- **Skin softness + finger crookedness together** amplify stubbornness/rigidity in personal preferences.
- **Knots present in Psychic Hand** = gateway to high spiritual attainment; **absence of knots** in Psychic Hand = comfort-seeking, low-exertion tendency.
- For **Mixed Hand**, always find the **dominant/major type** first, then layer in secondary-type traits individually.

### Special Signs / Markings Discussed
- **Fine vertical & horizontal lines on nails** — visible only under light/certain angle, not by touch; located on top of the nail surface (technique: examine nail surface under proper lighting).
